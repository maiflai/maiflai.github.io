A Dovecote for the 21st Century

The sauna is said to have been in use in Finland for over 2000 years. In relaxing the user's body and mind, it provides a psychological peace and contentment as well as physical relaxation. Some claim that the sauna relaxes symptoms of minor illnesses such as colds and clears the complexion.

In a variety of ways, the sauna experience will leave you feeling very much relaxed and alive.
