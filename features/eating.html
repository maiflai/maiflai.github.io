A Dovecote for the 21st Century

Thankfully there is no longer a need to select dinner from one of the nesting boxes. Instead, a fully-fitted kitchen has everything you'll need to survive.

The deceptively sized kitchen includes electric cooker and hob, dishwasher, fridge, microwave and high and low level units. A water softener is provided and a drinking water faucet is found next to the sink. Outside the kitchen is an alcove with washer / dryer machine.

A welcome pack is provided, including eggs, bread, milk, butter, jam & marmalade that can be tailored to your exact requirements - all you have to do is ask.
